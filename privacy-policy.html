Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to apps published by Simo Studio. It explains what information may be handled by those apps and how support enquiries are managed.

Information collected by apps

Simo Studio apps are intended to collect the minimum information needed for their stated purpose. Unless a specific app explains otherwise, the apps do not require users to create an account and do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

Advertising and third-party services

Some apps may use advertising, analytics, crash reporting, app store services, or other third-party tools. These services may process device information, diagnostics, identifiers, approximate location, or usage data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

Support contact

If you contact Simo Studio for support, your email address and message content will be used only to respond to the enquiry and maintain a record of the support conversation.

Children's privacy

Simo Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact Simo Studio so it can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when apps, platform requirements, or third-party services change. The date at the top of the page will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact simharuk@gmail.com.
